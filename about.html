SUBJECT TO CHANGE

My name is Mike and I am a pseudographer.

Though defined as 'false writing' or 'forgery', pseudography in this context acts as a mockery of photography. But not just photography in general, since this site is all about photography, but rather of the idea behind putting the word photography in connection with one's name to create a false impression of professionalism. Like so:

www.johndoephotography.com

And since I like to take a stand against such behavior and by no means consider myself a professional photographer (quite on the contrary, in fact), hence pseudography.
